New! J & S Pools Inc. is always searching for new ways to serve our customers.  We are excited to announce that we are teaming up with Pick Up Propane BBQ Tank Exchange Company, a division of Kamps Propane, a family owned California company located in Manteca, to offer you a new service.  We will be able to deliver or exchange barbecue propane tanks right to your pool area during our scheduled pool service!  No more running out of propane right in the middle of a family barbecue!  No more trying to find a gas station or a store that offers tank exchanges, and once you do, try finding someone to help you!

We know most of you probably have a gas barbecue or perhaps a propane heater and would find this service valuable.  We suggest if you don’t have a spare tank, now would be a good time to get one.  Please see our rate schedule below.  To get started give us a call at 297-7995 or order via email info@jspool.com. For our store customers or propane needed sooner than your service day our tank exchange program is also available for in-store pick-up!  Our store hours are Monday thru Friday, 8am to 6pm.  Hours are extended through the summer months.  So stop on by and let us help keep your grill hot!
